OverWatch

Overwatch is a team based game with two teams fighting each other to win the objective or game. There are 22 pickable characters and 13 different maps in the world of Overwatch. There are 5 different gamemodes in Overwatch, Quick Play, Competitive, Co-op vs. AI, Custom, and a weekly rotational fun game modes. This game was made by the gaming company Blizzard Entertainment, they also made famous games such as Hearthstone and Starcraft.
